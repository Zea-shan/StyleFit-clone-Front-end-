Why You Should Train at 5 AM: The Science Behind Morning Workouts

Research from the British Journal of Sports Medicine shows that morning exercisers burn up to 20% more body fat than those who train later. Early training spikes cortisol at its natural peak, primes your body for a fat-oxidizing state, and builds a level of mental fortitude that carries you through the toughest challenges of the day. By winning the morning, you win the day. This guide covers the biological adaptation phase, specific light exposure protocols to reset your internal clock, and the "No-Think" morning routine used by elite athletes. Real strength is built in the moments the world doesn't see. Motivation might get you started, but it is your unbreakable consistency that will ultimately carry you across the finish line of your transformation.
